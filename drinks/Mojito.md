
# Mojito

# Serving Size
20 drinks

# Ingredients

- White Rum
    20 - 1.5 oz shots
- Simple Syrup
    8 - 1.5 oz shots
- Lime Juice
    8 - 1.5 oz shots
- Fresh Mint
    3 cups loose
- Cut Limes
    2 limes
- Club Soda
    - 4L soda (2x 2L bottles OR 11 cans)
- Ice

# Steps
- Muddle Fresh Mint with a bit of Syrup
- Add all ingredients except Club Soda together
- Stir
- Add Club Soda
- Stir gently to minimally disturb carbonation
