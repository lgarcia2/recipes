# Lemon Chicken Marinade

Recipe made on
- 2023-07-02

## Ingredients

### Required
- Lemon Juice
    - 3-4 Tbsp
- Olive Oil
    - 3 Tbsp
- Salt
    - 1/4 tsp
- Pepper
    - to taste
- Garlic Powder
    - 1/2 tsp
- Onion Powder
    - 1/4 tsp
- Rosemary
    - 1/4 tsp


## Steps

- Mix all Ingredients
- Marinade Chicken in ingrediante

## Other Notes
- Should make ~1/2 cup, necessary for ~1lb chicken
